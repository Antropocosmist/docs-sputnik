# Advisers

**5,000,000 $SIGNA**L (**\~ 0.49%** **of the Total Supply)** will be distributed among **advisers** with vesting period of 1 year for:

* Assistance in integration with important infrastructure projects
* Getting valuable advice&#x20;
* Networking - helping with integration with important infrastructure projects

The rules apply to this distribution:&#x20;

* No more than **500,000** **$SIGNAL** per **Advisor**&#x20;
* The exact amount will depend on the Advisor's contribution to the development of the [Sputnik Network](../../../sputnik-network-app-chain/)&#x20;
* Any undistributed **$SIGNAL** will go to [Sputnik Foundation](../../../sputnik-foundation.md), or will go to the [Community Pool](../creating-pools/community-pool.md) - details on this will be clarified closer to the launch of the main network
