* If the [**Internal Record**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-record.md) does not contain information about usernames and/or email, [**Sputnik App**](./) creates an [**Internal Address**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-address.md) for the [**User**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/user.md), and records information about the connection of the userâ€™s [**Internal Address**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/internal-address.md) with the connected username or email&#x20;
* After this, the [**User**](../glossary-of-terms-and-scheme/user.md) gains access to the [**Personal Account**](personal-account.md)
